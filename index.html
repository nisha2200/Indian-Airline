What is Singapore "flexible dates" feature and why should I care?

Singapore flexible dates refer to the willingness to travel on different days to find cheaper flights, avoiding peak travel times like festivals and holidays. The season matters because off-peak times, such as the monsoon season (June to September), generally offer lower fares. Flexibility and season awareness can significantly reduce travel costs.

When is the best time to buy plane tickets - Last minute or in advance?

The best time to buy plane tickets is typically in advance, ideally 1-3 months before domestic flights and 2-8 months before international flights. Last-minute deals can sometimes be cheaper, but they're less reliable and often more expensive.

How does Singapore find such low flight prices?

Singapore find low flight prices by using flight comparison websites like Cleartrip, MakeMyTrip, and Skyscanner, taking advantage of airline sales and promotions, booking during off-peak seasons, and being flexible with travel dates.

How does Singapore flight Price Forecast tool help me choose the right time to buy?

The TASC 2 (Ticket-Age Search Chart) tool helps you choose the right time to buy flights by analyzing historical data to predict the best times for booking. It shows price trends over time, helping you identify when fares are likely to be at their lowest, ensuring you make cost-effective purchasing decisions.
